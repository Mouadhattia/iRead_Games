US-25: Daily Challenge with Multiple Words
As a player, I want the daily challenge to include a pack of three to five words per day, so that I can have a more engaging and extended gameplay experience.
Acceptance Criteria:
1.	Each daily challenge should consist of a pack of three or five words.
2.	Players must complete all words in the pack to finish the challenge.
3.	Players can complete the challenge at their own pace but must finish it within the same day for the results to be recorded.
4.	Scores should be calculated based on the total performance across all words in the pack.
5.	The daily challenge should reset every day at a fixed time (e.g., 12:00 AM local time).

US-26: Flexible Daily Challenge with Partial Scoring & Multi-Session Support
As a player, I want to play a daily challenge consisting of multiple words (3-5) and have the flexibility to complete it across multiple sessions within the same day, so that I can continue playing at my convenience while still earning partial scores.
Acceptance Criteria:
1.	Daily Challenge Structure:
o	Each daily challenge consists of 3 to 5 words selected for that day.
o	Players can complete the words in any order.
2.	Multi-Session Support:
o	Players can pause and resume the daily challenge at any time before midnight.
o	Previously completed words remain locked and cannot be replayed.
3.	Partial Scoring System:
o	If a player completes at least one word, they receive a score for that word.
o	The final score for the day is the sum of all completed word scores.
o	A bonus multiplier is applied if all words are completed.
4.	End-of-Day Rules:
o	The challenge resets at midnight with a new word pack.
o	Any incomplete words will not count towards achievements or win streaks.
o	Players can view their past incomplete games in the calendar but cannot replay them.
5.	Leaderboard & Statistics:
o	Scores are only ranked if at least 3 words are completed.
o	The player’s stats will track win rate, average score, and words completed per challenge.

As a player, I want to access a calendar showing daily challenges from the last month, so that I can play missed challenges and track my past performance.

US-26: Achievement System (Expanded)
As a player, I want an achievement system that rewards milestones such as winning streaks, quick wins, solving difficult words without hints, and completing consecutive daily challenges, so that I can stay motivated and track my progress.
Acceptance Criteria:
1.	The game should track and reward achievements for:
o	Winning streaks (e.g., 5, 10, 20 wins in a row).
o	Quick wins (solving a word in 3 guesses or less).
o	No hint wins (solving a word without using any hints).
o	Completing multiple daily challenges in a row (e.g., a 7-day streak of playing daily challenges).
o	Mastering all word lengths (winning at least one game for each word length from 3 to 7 letters).
2.	Achievements should be displayed in the player's profile.
3.	Players should be notified when unlocking a new achievement.
4.	Achievements should include visual badges or trophies for motivation.
5.	Players should be able to view all available achievements and track progress toward unlocking them.

US-27: Statistics Dashboard (Expanded with Daily Challenges)
As a player, I want to see a detailed statistics dashboard showing my overall and daily challenge performance, so that I can track my progress and compare my results over time.
Acceptance Criteria:
1.	The player’s statistics should include:
o	Win rate by word length (3-7 letters).

o	Best scores (from predefined words list).
o	Longest winning streak (for both regular and daily challenge games).

2.	The statistics should be updated automatically after each game.
3.	Players should be able to filter their statistics by game type (regular mode vs. daily challenge).

US-28: Practice Mode (Expanded with Customization Options)
As a player, I want a practice mode where I can play unlimited games with customizable settings, so that I can improve my skills without affecting my daily statistics.
Acceptance Criteria:
1.	Players should be able to select:
o	Word length (3-7 letters).
o	Number of guesses allowed (standard or custom).
o	Hint availability (enabled or disabled).
2.	Practice mode progress should not affect daily statistics.
3.	The player should receive performance feedback at the end of a practice session.
4.	The game should offer word definition hints, even in practice mode, to aid learning.
5.	Players should have an option to repeat words they previously struggled with (tracked based on past games).
